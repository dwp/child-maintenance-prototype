Graham and emily's payment agreement

Graham and Emily separated after 4 years of marriage. They have one child, Thomas, who lives with Emily most of the time.

Graham works different hours every week, so they found it difficult to agree a fixed amount for child maintenance. Instead, Emily and Graham went supermarket shopping for Thomas, buying all the basics and essentials for that week. They split this total in half, and Graham agreed to load that amount onto a pre-paid credit card every week, for Emily to do the shopping with.

Graham also offered to chip in if Thomas needs any specific things, like a new pair of shoes. He also sometimes gives Emily an extra contribution if he has worked a lot of hours in the week.

Emily understands that Graham’s hours are varied so she lets him have Thomas whenever he wants to. It’s important to her that Graham and Thomas continue to be a part of each other’s lives.
