Calculate child maintenance

Use this calculator to get a child maintenance calculation for your circumstances. You will be able to share the result with the child's other parent.

The receiving parent has main day-to-day care of the child. The paying parent does not have main day-to-day care.

The calculator is based on the rules used by the Child Maintenance Service.

Before you start you will need to know:

  • whether the paying parent gets any benefits or state pensions
  • the paying parent's total income, before tax is taken off
  • if any other children live with the paying parent
