Arrange child maintenance payments

Arranging payments yourselves

You can arrange payments yourselves, if you can agree. This is called a ’family-based arrangement’.

You agree how much the financial contributions should be and when they should be made.

No one else is involved, and it can be flexible to your circumstances.

Get help with arranging payments yourselves.

Using the Child Maintenance Service (CMS)

If you cannot agree payments yourselves, you can contact the CMS.

The CMS can work out how much child maintenance should be paid and set it up for you to make payments. There is a £20 fee for using the CMS, unless you are under 19, a victim of domestic abuse or live in Northern Ireland.

The £20 fee does not guarantee payments for child maintenance will be made.

If child maintenance payments are not made

If payments are not being made, CMS may be able to collect the amount and pay it. there will be fiurther fees for this.

Get help using the CMS.
