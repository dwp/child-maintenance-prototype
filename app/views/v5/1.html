Arrange child maintenance payments

Arranging payments yourselves

You can arrange payments yourselves, if you can agree. This is called a ’family-based arrangement’.

You agree how much the financial contributions should be and when they should be made.

No one else is involved, and it can be flexible to your circumstances.

Get more information about arranging payments yourselves.

Using the Child Maintenance Service (CMS)

If you cannot agree payments yourselves, you can contact the CMS.

The CMS will work out how much child maintenance should be paid and it can arrange for the money to be paid, if you want it to. There is a £20 fee for using the CMS, unless you are under 19, a victim of domestic abuse or in Northern Ireland.

The £20 fee does not guarantee an arrangement for child maintenance payments.

If child maintenance payments are not made

If you use the CMS, there might be other fees to pay on top of regular payments.

Get more information about using the CMS.
