Get help with my Child Maintenance options

Child maintenance is money to help pay for your child's everyday living costs when you are separated from the other parent.

{% from "inset-text/macro.njk" import govukInsetText %} {{ govukInsetText({ text: "Both parents are legally responsible for the financial costs of raising their children. " }) }}

Child maintenance payments are made by the parent who does not have day-to-day care of the child to the parent who has day-to-day care of the child.

Find out how to make a child maintenance arrangement

To use this service you must:

  • live in the UK
  • have a child under 20 in full time education
  • not have a court order that is less than 12 months old
