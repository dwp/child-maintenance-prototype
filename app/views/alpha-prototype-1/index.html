Child maintenance is financial support towards a child’s everyday living costs when the child doesn’t live with one or both parents.

Both parents are legally responsible for the financial costs of raising any children.

You can arrange regular payments of child maintenance between yourselves, or you may be able to apply to the Child Maintenance Service, if you can’t work together.

You can get help with your child maintenance options.
