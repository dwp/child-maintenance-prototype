What the Child Maintenance Service can do?

The Child Maintenance Service can:

  • try to find the other parent if you do not know where they live, to sort out child maintenance
  • try to sort out disagreements about parentage
  • work out how much child maintenance should be paid
  • arrange for the parent who does not have main day-to-day care of the child to pay child maintenance
  • pass payments on to the parent who has main day- to-day care of the child, if payments cannot be made directly
  • review the payment amounts when changes in parent’s circumstances are reported
  • review the payment amount every year
  • take action if payments are not made
