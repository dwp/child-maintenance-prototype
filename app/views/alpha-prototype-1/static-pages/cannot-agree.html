If you cannot agree

The Child Maintenance Service is for when you and the other parent cannot agree a child maintenance arrangement between yourselves.

You can apply if you are in the UK and you are:

  • the parent the child lives with
  • the parent the child does not live with
  • a grandparent or other guardian of the child
  • a child over 12 living in Scotland
