The cost of raising a child

This 'cost of raising your child' guide can help you think about the things children need and work out the true cost of bringing up your child.

Use it to split up the money you spend on your child into individual parts, from nappies for babies to entertainment for teenagers, and then work out who is best placed to pay for them.

When to use this guide

This guide is most useful for parents who are working together to arrange child maintenance.

If you’re thinking of making a private arrangement, the guide is a useful way of sorting out who’s going to pay for what and what the maintenance contribution should be.
