How to make a financial arrangement

If you choose to make a financial arrangement, both parents will need to agree:

  • how much financial contributions will be
  • when they will be paid
  • how payments will be made

You could also agree to review or change your arrangement in the future.

You can view how Claire and Mark made a financial arrangement.(opens in new window or tab)

If you need someone to help you to reach an agreement, ask a friend or family member, or find a local mediator (opens in new window or tab) to support you.

Many separated parents write down what they have agreed to, so they have something to refer to.
