You are not eligible to apply to the Child Maintenance Service

The Child Maintenance Service can only make child maintenance arrangements for children who are either:

  • under 16
  • under 20 and in full-time education up to A level or equivalent
  • under 20 and living with a parent who has registered for Child Benefit for them

Some separated parents make their own arrangements to support older children.
