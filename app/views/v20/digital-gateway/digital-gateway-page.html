Before you apply

If you need a calculation to make your own child maintenance arrangement, use the online calculator to work out a fair amount, and access guides which can help you and the other parent to work together.

The online calculator is based on the same rules as the Child Maintenance Service and is free to use.

Apply to the Child Maintenance Service

To apply, you will need to pay a non-refundable application fee of £20 (unless you are a victim of domestic abuse, live in Northern Ireland or are under 19). Payment of the fee does not guarantee that payments will be made.

If you cannot provide enough information for us to contact the other parent, we may not be able to open a child maintenance case for you. Your application fee will not be refunded.
