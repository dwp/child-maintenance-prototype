Are the children in full time approved education?

Education must be full-time (more than an average of 12 hours a week supervised or course-related work experiance) and can include: A levels or similar, for example, Pre U, International Baccalaureate, Scottish Highers, NVQs and other vocational qualifications up to level 3 home education, if started before your child turned 16.

Traineeships in England courses are not approved if paid for by an employer or 'advanced', for example a unibversity degree or BTEC Higher National Certificate.
