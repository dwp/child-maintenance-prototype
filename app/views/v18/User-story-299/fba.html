Calculate child maintenance

Use this calculator to work out an amount of child maintenance for your children.

The online calculator uses the same basic rules as the Child Maintenance Service – a government scheme for parents who cannot make their own child maintenance arrangements.

You can use the calculation as a starting point for making your own arrangement with the other parent.

You’ll need to know:

  • whether the parent who will be paying child maintenance gets any benefits, State Pension, or income
  • if any children live with the paying parent most of the time

Information you provide will only be used for your calculation and will not be stored.
