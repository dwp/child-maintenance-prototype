You are eligible to apply to the Child Maintenance Service for John

John must be under 16 years old, or under 20 years old and in full time approved education to be eligible to apply to the child maintenance service.

What is full time approved education?
Education must be a full-time (more than average of 12 hours a week supervised study or course-related work experience) and can include: A-Levels or similar, for example Pre-U, International Baccalaureate Scottish Highers NVQs and other vocational qualifications up to level 3 Home education, if started before your child turned 16 Traineeships in England Courses are not approved if paid for by an employer or ‘advanced’, for example a university degree or BTEC Higher National Certificate.
