To make a calculation we need to know the income of the parent who will be paying child maintenance.

If you don’t know the income of the paying parent, the cost of raising your child guide (downloads a PDF) may help you to work out how much maintenance your child might need.

If the parent who does not live with the child receives a benefit or State Pension, the Child Maintenance Service calculates a ‘Flat rate’ of £7 per week.

If you cannot agree child maintenance between yourselves, the Child Maintenance Service can help.
